Sketti!

Description:

Sketti (or "spaghetti" if your not fun), is a super easy meal that can be made in under 30 minutes. It is always a goto meal when we are short of time, or just cannot be bothered to make something else.

One of the best parts is this recipe is that it is great to make even with kids because you can almost hand off the whole process to them!

Ingredients:

  • Sketti
  • Ground Beef
  • Sketti Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps:

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a rolling boil
  2. Add noodles to water
  3. Brown ground beef in a medium skillet
  4. Add sketti sauce to beef and bring to a simmer
  5. When sketti is tender drain and add to pasta bowls
  6. Top with sauce and cheese
