Tacos!

Description:

Tacos are a family favorite that always gathers a crowd and never fails to to impress. The ingredients are cheap, the prep work is minimal, and the process can be completed in under 30 minutes.

One of the best parts is this recipe is that it is great to make even with with kids because there are fun things they can do as well!

Ingredients:

  • Taco shells
  • Ground beef
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Sour Cream
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Taco Sauce
  • Shredded Cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  2. Brown ground beef in a medium skillet
  3. Season beef with taco seasoning, add some sauce, and set to simmer
  4. While beef simmers, place taco shells on a tray and put them in the oven
  5. Shred lettuce
  6. Dice tomatoes and onion
  7. Place lettuce, tomatoes, and onion in small bowls
  8. Assemble tacos while fighting off your children!
